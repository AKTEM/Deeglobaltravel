FAQ

Your Questions Answered

What is an H-1B Teaching Work Permit?

An H-1B Teaching Work Permit(USA) allows foreign nationals to work as teachers in specialty occupations requiring a bachelor's degree or higher. It is employer-sponsored and typically valid for up to six years.

What is a USA Study Permit?

A USA Study Permit, allows foreign students to study at accredited U.S. institutions while maintaining full-time enrollment.

How do I apply for USA Study Permit?

Contact Us because our professional team is ready to handle the entire process for you through our partner institutions/colleges in USA, and provide expert travel consultation to make your study abroad experience very smooth.

What is a Dublin Study Permit?

A Dublin Study Permit, allows foreign nationals to study at accredited institutions in Dublin, Ireland, for the duration of their academic program.

How do I apply for Dublin Study Permit?

We assist in securing a Dublin work permit through our trusted partner colleges. Our team handles the entire application process on your behalf, starting with obtaining your offer of admission.

How do I apply for H1B Teaching Permit?

Reach out to us for proper guidance on the process and requirements for an H-1B teaching permit. Let us make your journey to the U.S. a success
